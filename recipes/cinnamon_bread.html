Cinnamon Bread

This cinnamon quick bread has a sweet cinnamon topping that's swirled into the batter for a marbled effect. It's a delicious way to start your morning!

Cinnamon Bread Ingredients

These are the ingredients you’ll need to make this cinnamon bread recipe:

For the Bread

  • all-purpose flour
  • white sugar
  • buttermilk
  • eggs
  • vegetable oil
  • vanilla extract
  • baking powder
  • ground cinnamon
  • salt
  • baking soda

For the topping

  • white sugar
  • butter
  • margarine
  • ground cinnamon

How to Make Cinnamon Bread

You’ll find the full, step-by-step recipe below — but here’s a brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade cinnamon bread:

  1. Make the batter and pour it into a prepared loaf pan.
  2. Make the topping and sprinkle it over the batter.
  3. Use a paring knife to swirl batter for a marbled effect.
  4. Bake until a toothpick comes out clean.

How to Store Cinnamon Bread

Store the bread in a bread box, in a storage container, or wrapped in storage wrap at room temperature for up to five days. Avoid refrigeration, as this will dry out the bread.
